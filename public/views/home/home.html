The Husky Band

I joined the Husky Marching Band back in the fall of 2012. Never in my wildest dreams did I ever think that I would be able to perform for crowds up to 70,000 people! It was definitely nerve-wracking but well worth it.

Transferring

In the fall of 2015 I found myself at an impasse. I was expected to graduate the following spring with a degree in a subject matter that I wasn't too passionate about. I looked into a variety of options and thought deeply about what I really enjoyed doing. Well, I love playing video games and I'd always wanted to create a game of my own. I had taken Computer Science courses in 2014 but I never really considered it as a career path. I just wanted to understand what some of my friends were talking about. I decided why not give Computer Science a shot! I was a bit too late to apply for the CSE department at University of Washington, Seattle and didn't want to live too close to home by attending University of Washington, Bothell. So I contacted the advisors over at the University of Washington, Tacoma and set up a meeting to check out the campus and program. A couple months later I began my journey as a Computer Science student.
